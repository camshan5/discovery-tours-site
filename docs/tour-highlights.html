Your Trip.

With 4 days of touring Washington D.C., you will have the chance to visit the best this city has to offer. Here is a preview of some of what you might see.

All Museums Memorials Landmarks
World War II Memorial

Honors the more than 400,000 who died in WWII.

Ford's Theater

Sit in the theater seats as park rangers relive the day Lincoln was assassinated.

National Air & Space Museum

Neil Armstrong’s spacesuit from the Apollo 11 Moon landing is on display for the first time in 13 years!

Vietnam Veterans Memorial

See the names of 58,318 Americans who gave their lives serving their country.

Museum of Natural History

Discover everything dinosaurs to the Hope diamond here.

Korean War Memorial

Life size bronzes walk wearily from a battlefield depicting the hardship of war

Washington Monument

Take a ride in the glass elevator for a panoramic view of Washington.

White House
Library of Congress

Jefferson Memorial

Thomas Jeffierson stands in the rotunda surrounded by his famous writings.

Supreme Court

The highest court in the land is an impressive landmark across the street from the capitol.

Franklin Delano Roosevelt Memorial
