Trip Participation

The cost of this year’s trip will be $1320.00*. The price includes round trip airfare, hotel accommodations, and all meals, entrance fees to highlighted locations, ground transportation and gratuities. While this is not a school sponsored trip, the students are chaperoned by school district personnel at all times. The trip is scheduled for Sunday, May 30 through Wednesday, June 2 or Monday, May 31 through Thursday, June 3. A meeting will be held in mid-May to inform parents of final details about packing, assigned flights, medical release forms, etc. We look forward to your participation.

Here are terms in regards to payments and schedule:

February 15, 2021

March 15, 2021

May 15, 2021

Checks or money orders are made payable to Discovery Tours and mailed to PO Box 23, League City, TX, 77574. Payments may also be submitted through Venmo. Venmo payments should include the child’s name and school in the memo line as well as a contact number.

Participants have the opportunity to purchase Trip Cancellation Plan* or TCP. The cost of the plan is $200. It must be paid with the initial registration payment and may not be added at a later date. Participants can prorate the payment plan by adding $50 to each of the four scheduled payments. The TPP allows a participant to cancel the trip for full refund up to 24 hours before departure. A TCP form or must be completed at the time of registration.

Students will be required to have satisfactory conduct in order to participate. If a child’s conduct is deemed unacceptable, payment will be returned. Discovery Tours holds the right not to accept a participant on the field trip based on the safety and welfare of the trip as a whole.

All participants will be required to have valid medical insurance before the departure of the trip. Those participants not insured may purchase medical trip insurance at 1-800-826-4919.

Adult participants who wish to upgrade their rooms may do so for an additional fee. The trip is priced at four participants to a room. The fee for the room upgrade should be divided equally between the five payments. Please initial room type choice.

Quad Room (4 adults, 2 double beds and a pull out) | No additional charge

Triple Room (3 adults, 2 double beds and a pull out) | $56.00 each/ $14 per payment

Double Room* (2 adults, 2 double beds or a king and a pull out) | $168.00 each/ $42 per payment

Single Room* (1 adult, king bed) | $504.00/ $124 per payment

*Double and single rooms are subject to availability.
