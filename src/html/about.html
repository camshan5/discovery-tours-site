About Us

We are all educators with many years of working with students in the classroom and on tour. We pride ourselves with knowing how to reach every student to make their travel experience rewarding.

41 years of working with only local students in the greater Houston, TX area has enabled us to dedicate our time to the students we teach. We have not grown beyond this because the founding purpose was to help the teachers we work with to best enhance their students' educational opportunities
